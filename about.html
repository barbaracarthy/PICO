Our Mission:
PICO has two goals, To end extreme Computer illiteracy and promote shared prosperity in a sustainable way

Vision:
To make everyone capable of standing out in the 22nd generation.

We are a young and talented group of engineers dedicated to making PICO accesible to everyone whole over the world.

PICO was establised in the year 1978 by Sir Felix Pascal, a Chinese explorer and since then we have contributed to the world's growing IT sector.
